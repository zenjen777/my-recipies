Curry Chicken

Ingredients
1 Chicken
2 tbs Curry Powder
4 Tomatoes
2 tsp Salt
2 cups Water
1 cup yogurt
2 tbs cayenne

Directions
Put water on to boil and add salt
After boiling reduce to simmer
Add curry powder and tomatoes
Stir fry chicken
Add cooked chicken to simmer
To make extra sauce combine yogurt and cayenne.
